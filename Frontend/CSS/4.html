7. Stacking Contexts (CRITICAL!)

z-index only compares elements within the same stacking context. Child can't escape parent's context!

‚ùå Why z-index: 9999 doesn't work

z-index: 1

Child with z-index: 9999 is still behind, because its parent's stacking context (z-index: 1) is below the other parent (z-index: 2).

What creates stacking contexts?
  • ‚úì position + z-index (not auto)
  • ‚úì opacity < 1
  • ‚úì transform, filter, perspective
  • ‚úì position: fixed or sticky
  • ‚úì flex/grid items with z-index
  • ‚úì isolation: isolate
